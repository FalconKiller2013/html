The Toyota Supra MK4 is a Japanese sports car that was produced from 1993 to 2002. It is known for its performance, design, and modifiability. Features: Engine: The MK4 has a 3.0-liter twin-turbo straight-six engine, also known as the 2JZ-series. Horsepower: The MK4 can produce up to 320 horsepower. Acceleration: The MK4 can go from 0 to 100 km/h in about 4.9 seconds. Top speed: The MK4 can reach speeds of up to 277 km/h. Roof: The MK4 was available as a hardtop, with or without a sunroof, or with a removable targa roof. Body kit: Many owners added aftermarket body kits to their MK4s, including those featured in the Fast and Furious movies.
