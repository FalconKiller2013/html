Supra mk4

The Toyota Supra MK4 (1993-2002) is renowned for its powerful 2JZ engine and robust drivetrain. Key mechanisms include the twin-turbocharged 2JZ-GTE engine, available six-speed manual (Getrag) or four-speed automatic transmissions, and a four-wheel independent suspension system. The car also features optional ABS with lateral G-sensing, a retractable front spoiler, and high-penetration headlamps.

Ferrari-F80

The F80 uses a hybrid powertrain, consisting of a 3.0L twin-turbocharged Tipo F163 CF 120° V6 petrol engine derived from the Ferrari 499P, and three electric motors.

Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko's powertrain features a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V8 engine and a revolutionary 9-speed Light Speed Transmission (LST). The engine utilizes a flat-plane crankshaft, producing a visceral sound and even firing across engine banks

Aston Martin Valkyrie AMR PRO

The AMR Pro uses the same 6.5-litre naturally-aspirated V12 engine as used in the Valkyrie road car without the KERS system. The engine will also be modified, which means the AMR Pro will have up to 820 kW (1,100 hp)(estimated 870–970 kW (1,160–1,300 hp)); which is more than its road-legal counterpart.

Mclaren F1

The McLaren F1's innovative mechanisms included its carbon fiber monocoque chassis, dihedral doors, and airbrake, all designed to optimize performance and driver experience. The car also featured a complex engine with features like variable valve timing and a dual fuel injector system.

GTR R34

The R34 Nissan Skyline GT-R's mechanism revolves around its powerful RB26DETT engine, advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system, and sophisticated Super HICAS four-wheel steering. These features, combined with its robust build and tuner-friendly nature, contribute to its iconic status.

GTR R35

The Nissan GT-R R35, often called Godzilla, utilizes a sophisticated mechanism centered around its VR38DETT engine and GR6 dual-clutch transmission. The engine features a dual overhead camshaft (DOHC) valve train with variable valve timing control, while the transmission is a transaxle design with dual clutches for rapid gear changes.
